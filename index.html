Selling Your Practice

We work with owner‑managed accounting and advisory firms that have been carefully built over many years and are now approaching a transition point.

Our process is designed specifically for firms with $500k–$5M in annual fees across the US and UK, where the owner wants a clean, discreet exit — without unsettling staff, clients, or the local community.

This is typically a good fit if you:

  • Are considering retirement or stepping back in the next 6–24 months
  • Have long‑standing client relationships you want protected
  • Employ an experienced team you care deeply about
  • Use modern or partially modernised cloud systems
  • Want clarity, speed, and discretion — not a drawn‑out sale process

If that sounds like you, we should talk.
